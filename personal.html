Personal Life

Family

I have one brother and one sister and I have been married to my beautiful wife for seven years. We have a lot of family get togethers and are very close.

Friends

Friends are people that I choose very carefully. I only consider a handful of people true friends and they are considered family to me. We have a lot of fun, we've been through a lot and we are always there for each other.

Pets

To most, pets are just animals, but they are so much more to my wife and I. To us, they are our children. We have two cats and four dogs. Two Dobermans, a Dalmatian and a Chocolate Lab. They can be a handful, but I couldn't imagine how empty our home would be without them.
